Sleep Tips

Sleep tips are essential for improving sleep quality, boosting overall health and enhancing daily productivity.

Maintain a Consistent Sleep Schedule

Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day to regulate your body’s internal clock.

Follow a Relaxing Bedtime Routine

Engage in calming activities like reading or taking a warm bath to signal your body it’s time to sleep.

Limit Screen Time Before Bed

Avoid screens at least an hour before bedtime to prevent blue light from disrupting melatonin production.

Create a Comfortable Sleep Environment

Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet, and invest in a good mattress and pillow.

Watch Your Food and Drink Intake

Avoid heavy meals, caffeine, and alcohol before bed as they can disrupt your sleep.

Stay Physically Active During the Day

Exercise regularly to promote better sleep, but avoid vigorous workouts close to bedtime.
